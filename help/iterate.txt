[1;4miterate[0m

Applies f to x0 n times, returning f^n(x0). Runs as a flat internal loop
(O(1) stack), so it scales to millions of steps where recursion would
overflow. The safe, scalable way to time-step a simulation.

[1mExamples:[0m
[0m> iterate(x -> 2*x, 1, 10)
result = 1024
> iterate(v -> (v[1], -v[0]), (1,0), 4)
result = [1, 0]
