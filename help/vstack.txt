[1;4mvstack[0m

Stacks two arrays vertically (row-wise). Ranks are promoted: a 1-D vector
is treated as a single row, and a scalar as a 1x1 block, so a vector can be
stacked onto a matrix. Column counts must match.

[1mExamples:[0m
[0m> vstack((1,2,3), (4,5,6))
result =
⎡ 1  2  3 ⎤
⎣ 4  5  6 ⎦
> vstack((1,2;3,4), (5,6))
result =
⎡ 1  2 ⎤
⎢ 3  4 ⎥
⎣ 5  6 ⎦
[0m
